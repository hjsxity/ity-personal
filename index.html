Poetry

A little poetry from me

Do you know?

I really like poetry, for me poetry is the most enjoyable way of expression.
We are all free to condemn, praise and express our feelings without normative restrictions.
Poetry is not just a medium of expression.
Whatever its form, poetry is a masterpiece that will last forever.
Moreover, if many people read the poem, they will definitely remember the poem.
Not just poetry, I like phrases too.
I don't know what inspired me to find it, all kinds of beautiful word arrangements. Maybe from all God's blessings? Both nature, humans, feelings and the environment around me.
Some say “ity, you have this talent”, but I think, I'm not there yet-talent-no.
Unfortunately, I can't even say it directly through words, even through my mouth. I can only do it through writing, more precisely, and through my heart alone.
Here, this time I will only show a little of my work, mine, mine. Hahaha, to be honest, I'm embarrassed.
